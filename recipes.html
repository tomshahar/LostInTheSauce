Orange and Coffee Chocolate Chip Cookies

30 Minutes

18 Cookies

Ingredients

1 cup Unsalted Butter
1 cup White Sugar
1 cup Brown Sugar
2 Large Eggs
1.5 tsp Vanilla Extract
0.5 tsp Orange Extract
2 tsp Coffee
1 tsp Baking Soda
1.5 tsp Salt
3 cups Flour

Instructions

Preheat oven to 360 degrees. If you have convection, use it. Soften butter for 15 seconds in the microwave. In the bowl of a stand mixer, cream together the butter and both sugars until fully combined. Add the eggs, and mix until just incorporated.

Add the vanilla, orange, coffee, baking soda, and salt. Mix until fully combined.

Add flour in iterations of 1 cup. The dough should be slightly sticky when pressed. Take a spoonful and tilt it; the dough should stick to the spoon completely, not fall down. You may find that less that 3 cups of flour gives better dough, so add the last cup in smaller iterations.

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper, and scoop balls of dough of about 2.5 tbsp each, 6 cookies per batch. Bake for between 8 and 10 minutes, then let cool for at least 2 minutes on the baking sheet.
